Trans People Matter.


On Sunday October 21st, word broke that the Trump administration had plans in place to define gender as male and female, and assigned at birth. This is a gross erasure of the hundreds of thousands of trans people that live in the United States.

Below are some links to information on registering to vote, and organizations you can donate to in order to support the trans community under the Trump administration. Show the white house that we will not stand for policy based on hatred and intolerance.




Register to vote.

The very first thing you can do is ensure you are registered to vote. requirements vary from state to state, but you can click here to go to to a site that will help you register to vote in your state of residence.





Find your polling place.

Once you're registered to vote, ensure you know where your polling place is. Click on the icon to the left to be redirected to a site that will help you find it. Lyft is also offering free rides on election day, so take advantage of that if needed.





Donate.

There are many good organizations you can donate to to support the trans community. Click here to get some more information on some of them, and find links to donate.



Speak up.

Click here to call your representative and let them know that you will not stand for the erasure of the trans community in the United States.





Get involved. (coming soon)

If you aren't in a position to donate money, you can volunteer your time. Click here to be connected with volunteer opportunities.





Talk to someone.

Need to talk to somebody about what's going on? The trans lifeline is a great resource. Click above to be redirected to their contact page.
